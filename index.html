ABOUT

Wil la mootk

On April 16, 2014 we evolved from Children’s Mental Health and became Wil la mootk Counseling Center. Wil la mootk translates to “Place of Healing”. We are an internationally accredited clinic for outpatient behavioral health services. We are a team of professionals that strive earnestly to improve individually and collectively that we may be worthy of our reflection.

Exciting

News!

On February 22, 2023, Wil La Mootk Counseling Center became the first tribally owned and operated Child Placement Agency in the State of Alaska and the Nation!

This historical and monumental event is just first of many for Wil La Mootk Counseling Center.

We will continue to demonstrate our commitment to our quality of services on the highest level And embrace the concept of whole-person client centered healing.
