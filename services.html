MENTAL HEALTH ASSESSMENT

Wil La Mootk provides counseling services for individuals and families. We utilize a strength-based approach, helping clients utilize their own talents and abilities in a collaborative journey of health and healing. We are your invited guest and are honored to walk alongside you in your journey of healing and change.
